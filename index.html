Welcome

Our mission is to help community services, non profits, and low income individuals obtain affordable vacant land to build a home on.
We are commitmented to communities, and are active members of growing number on community networks, including the Assets and Opprotunity Network, and the I'm Home.



We beleive in an individuals, not including corportions, rights to information. We data responsibly in accordance with each state Public Records Acts, and providing the most useful data we are provided to others. We keep in mind the ongoing conversation about transparency vs anonymity and it goes without saying, use discretion with the protecting privacy.
