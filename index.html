Equity in Open Data

Welcome and thanks for visiting. We fight for low income individuals* and their rights to affordably obtain intellectual property in accordance with statewide public records acts. Read more about us by clicking here


We're active members in networks and communities aligned with this purpose such as "Assets and Opportunity Network", "I'm Home", "Be Your Own Contractor Training", and "Straw Bale Education".


A project we're currently working on is a data abstraction service for distressed properties in tax lien investing. We aim to assist low income individuals to legally obtain affordable vacant land to develop, giving owners the ability to liquidate their distressed asset(s), prior to counties auctioning the properties through companies like bid4assets.com. The results of the efforts help counties collect taxes more quickly, preventing properties from the additional cost burdens of foreclosure and liquidation services. This is why a handful of governing entities provide this information in real time for free on their website.


A current effort in our project aims to collect information on the over 3000 counties, cities, municipalities, local districts, and towns in North America.
